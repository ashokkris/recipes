Ingredients:
 - 4 regular or persian cumcumbers
 - 2 green chilis
 - 2 tablespoons lime juice
 - 1 tablespoon sesame oil
 - 1/2 teaspoon black mustard seeds
 - 1/2 teaspoon fenugreek
 - 1/4 teaspoon salt
 - A few cilantro leaves


Directions:
 1. Peel the cucumbers and cut them into small cubes of same size. Place in bowl
 2. Heat sesame oil in small sauce, then add mustard seeds and fenugreek
 3. When mustard seeds start to pop add cut green chilis and stop heating
 4. Add the heated oil and its contents to the cut cucumbers
 5. Add salt and mix evenly
 6. Add lime juice and mix
 7. Garnish with cilantro leaves
